Problem 1: Unit Conversions (12 points)

Easy
12 pts

A carpet is to be installed in a room whose length is measured to be 13.71 m and whose width is measured to be 4.46 m. Find the area of the room.

Hint

Area = length × width. Pay attention to significant figures in your final answer.

Problem 2: Volume Calculation (12 points)

Easy
12 pts

An object in the shape of rectangular parallelepiped measures 2.0 in × 3.5 in × 6.5 in. Determine the volume of the object in m³.

Hint

Volume = length × width × height. Convert inches to meters first (1 in = 0.0254 m).

Problem 3: Significant Figures (12 points)

Medium
12 pts

A rectangular plate has a length of (21.3 ± 0.2) cm and a width of (9.8 ± 0.1) cm. Calculate the area of the plate including its uncertainty.

Hint

For multiplication, the relative uncertainty is the sum of the relative uncertainties of the factors. Relative uncertainty = (uncertainty / value).

Problem 4: SI Prefixes (12 points)

Medium
12 pts

Express the following using appropriate SI prefixes:

(a) 0.00085 L
(b) 5.44 × 10⁻¹¹ g
(c) 73,000,000 m
(d) 9.450 s

Hint

Common SI prefixes: milli (10⁻³), micro (10⁻⁶), nano (10⁻⁹), pico (10⁻¹²), kilo (10³), mega (10⁶), giga (10⁹).

Problem 5: Dimensional Analysis (13 points)

Medium
13 pts

Analyze the following equations to determine whether dimensionally correct or not:

(a) v = u + ax

where v and u are velocities, a is acceleration, and x is distance

(b) y = (2m)cos(kx) where k = 2m⁻¹

where y and x are lengths, m is mass, and k has dimension of inverse length

Hint

Check if all terms in the equation have the same dimensions. Remember that trigonometric functions are dimensionless.

Problem 6: Vector Addition (13 points)

Hard
13 pts

Vector A has a magnitude of 10 units and makes 60° with the positive x-axis. Vector B has a magnitude of 5 units and is directed along the negative x-axis. Find the vector:

(a) sum A + B
(b) difference A - B

Hint

Break vectors into components. A = (10cos60°, 10sin60°), B = (-5, 0). Then add/subtract components.

Problem 7: Vector Operations (13 points)

Hard
13 pts

Find the cross product of A × B where A = 2i + 3j, B = -i + 2j.

Hint

For 2D vectors in the xy-plane, the cross product gives a vector in the z-direction: A × B = (AₓBᵧ - AᵧBₓ)k.

Show Hint

Problem 8: Physical Applications (13 points)

Hard
13 pts

A ship is steaming due east at a speed of 12 m/s. A passenger runs across the deck at a speed of 5 m/s toward north. What is the resultant velocity of the passenger relative to the sea?

Hint

The resultant velocity is the vector sum of the ship's velocity and the passenger's velocity relative to the ship.
